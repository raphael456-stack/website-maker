What is the Sustainable Development Goals?


The link of The Sustainable Development Goals


The Sustainable Development Goals are a universal call of action to end the environmental, social and economic issues that we are still excperiencing today for the betterment of the people and our world. It is established in 2015, founded by the United Nations, have the 17 Goals that are all interconnected and aiming to achieve it all by the end of 2030. One of the Goals that I'm to discuss is the SDG 1: No Poverty


The link of SDG 1: No Poverty


SDG 1: No Poverty is the first goal to end all forms of poverty everywhere by 2030. The goal is to solve the poverty issue by giving social protection who are poor and empowering them with equal rights to economic resource and services.
